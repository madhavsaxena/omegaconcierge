HOSPITAL CONCIERGE

Many of Omega’s earliest clients were hospitals, so our employees
know how to serve patients and staff alike.

Learn More

We can offer your hospital any combination of the following services, or we would be happy to provide all of them.

PATIENT CONCIERGE

While patients are at your facility, our staff pays them a daily visit to enhance the care they receive. We can handle everyday tasks, which allow your staff to focus on the medical care, and we can show our true New Orleans hospitality and make sure your patients have the best possible experience.
On a typical day, our concierges:
  • Adjust the thermostat
  • Fetch an additional pillow
  • Pick up prescriptions
  • Coordinate pet care
  • Book travel for family members coming to visit
We’ve also been known to:
  • Set up video conferencing for a soldier stationed in Iraq to meet his newborn baby
  • Decorate for and throw a birthday party for a young patient who was in the hospital on his birthday
Whatever your patients need, Omega’s patient concierge services reduce the demand on hospital staff and improve each patient’s overall experience at your hospital.

EMPLOYEE CONCIERGE

Doctors, nurses, and other staff are the greatest asset your hospital has. Providing your staff with employee concierge services can improve morale, increase focus on your patients, and retain your valuable employees.

Click here to learn more about employee concierge services that we provide to hospitals and a variety of other industries.

Valet Services

Our valet attendants offer a professional first impression for those coming to your hospital. They are courteous and bring the same care-focused mindset as our concierge staff.

Guest Services

Our guest services staff welcome your guests with a smile. They are hospitable, friendly, respectful of privacy, and they help with way-finding and other needs. We also offer access control for Labor and Delivery, putting an emphasis on patient privacy and safety.

Courier Services

For our hospital clients, our Courier Service transport items promptly from your location to the required destination. We deliver packages, letters, files, and items with swift care and reliability.
